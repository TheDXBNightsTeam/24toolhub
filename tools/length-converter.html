What is a Length Converter?

A length converter is an essential online tool that converts measurements between different length units. Whether you're working on a construction project, studying science, traveling internationally, or simply need to understand measurements in different systems, our free length converter provides instant and accurate conversions between metric and imperial units.

Supported Length Units

Metric Units

  • Millimeters (mm): The smallest metric unit, commonly used for precise measurements
  • Centimeters (cm): 10 millimeters, used for everyday measurements
  • Meters (m): The base unit of length in the metric system
  • Kilometers (km): 1000 meters, used for long distances

Imperial Units

  • Inches (in): 1/12 of a foot, commonly used in the US
  • Feet (ft): 12 inches, used for height and room dimensions
  • Yards (yd): 3 feet, used in sports and construction
  • Miles (mi): 5280 feet, used for long distances
  • Nautical Miles: Used in aviation and maritime navigation

How to Use the Length Converter

  1. Enter the value you want to convert in the "From" field
  2. Select the unit you're converting from in the dropdown
  3. Select the unit you want to convert to in the "To" dropdown
  4. Click "Convert" to see the result
  5. Use "Copy Result" to copy the converted value

Common Length Conversions

  • 1 meter = 3.28084 feet
  • 1 kilometer = 0.621371 miles
  • 1 inch = 2.54 centimeters
  • 1 foot = 0.3048 meters
  • 1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers
  • 1 yard = 0.9144 meters
